Class: GrowFadeBubble

GrowFadeBubble(options)

Scale the element from 0% to 100% of the current size (takes growTime seconds). Synchronously, the element fades from 100% to 0%. The fade will delay for delayTime seconds since the starts of the animation and will take fadeTime seconds. Since the animination is timed, once the animination completes, the element will be removed.
