Class: TailBubble

TailBubble(options)

Make and position HTMLElements around the target element. If the target element is moving (for example: is manipulated by a MoveBubble), the new HTMLElements will be tailing after the target element. The positions of new HTMLElements will be bounded by the size of the board. The total time of the period of the animation is indicated by the period
